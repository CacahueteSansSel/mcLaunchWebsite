Create instances

On mcLaunch, you can create instances to separate your modded

survival modpack from your vanilla up-to-date Minecraft.

mcLaunch support vanilla Minecraft and modloaders such as

Forge, Fabric, Quilt and even NeoForge !

Import from anywhere*

mcLaunch supports importing modpacks (and installing mods)

from CurseForge and Modrinth


*at least it's our goal

Free & Open Source
